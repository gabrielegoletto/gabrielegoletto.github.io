About me

I am a Research Scientist at the Spatial AI Lab at Microsoft in Zurich, led by Professor Marc Pollefeys. Previously, I was an Ellis PhD student at the Polytechnic University of Turin, working on egocentric video understanding. I was advised by Professor Barbara Caputo and co-advised by Professor Dima Damen.

Personal Interests

I’m passionate about trail running, hiking, and skiing. Beyond physical activities, I also enjoy playing music. I practiced classical guitar for many years and I am now expanding my skills by learning the piano.
